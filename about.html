About PagirEd

PagirEd is an innovative EdTech startup committed to turning shared ideas into tangible skills. We believe in hands‑on, project‑based learning that prepares learners for real‑world challenges. Whether you're a beginner or a professional, our courses are designed to help you gain practical experience, connect with industry experts, and earn an MSME‑recognized certification.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between learning and employment by providing learners with real projects, expert mentorship, and a supportive community. We focus on making every learner industry‑ready, confident, and capable of showcasing their skills to employers worldwide.
